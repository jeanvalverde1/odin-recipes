Fried Corn

Fried corn is a quick and easy snack that can be done in less than 6 minutes.

  • Corn
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  1. First wash the corn kernerls.
  2. Second, heat up the pan and the oil.
  3. Once the oiul is hot, place the corn and let it fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove corn and salt to taste.
